Detailed Description

Polygon.

A plane figure that is bounded by a finite chain of straight line segments closing in a loop to form a closed polygonal chain or circuit. These segments are called its edges, and the points where two edges meet are the polygon's vertices.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polygon
